Hygiene is Important!

It has come to our knowledge that a lot of students don't take care of their hygiene as much as they should. This makes for an rather filthy and unpleasant workspace and that is not acceptable. We in the schoolboard have because of this created a rule which says "If any student gets more than 3 warnings regarding their hygiene, they will be suspended for a week. Repeated incidents doubles the time suspended. When reaching 4 months the student will be expelled and not be given a refund on their schoolarship." We sincerely hope that this will help with the issue at hand.
